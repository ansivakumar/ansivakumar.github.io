Arun Narenthiran Sivakumar

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), advised by Prof. Girish Chowdhary focusing on vision and learning based autonomy for agricultural robots. Broadly, I am interested in developing and deploying field robotic systems for precision agriculture and environmental monitoring. Outside of research, I enjoy travelling, and perfecting my biryani recipes.
